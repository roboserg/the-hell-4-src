I have run into a problem. After the number of bosses exceeded 255, boss in slot for number 256 boss didn't work correctly and caused various bugs in-game. This slot is unusable. Absolutely. This slot is at offset B1270

After this boss slots kind of repeat previous boss slot names. In SP mode after activating a boss in such a slot, saving the game, and then loading the game, this boss' name will morph into his -256 slots corresponding boss name. Thus, my recently added bosses morphed into the butcher, the defiler (this one even dropped crypt map after dying!), aym lord of torment, etc.

I have a workaround plan on this matter.

Mark all the bosses with the same names (Xorene, Harlequin, etc.) and place them so that if their name morphs it would morph into the very same name.
