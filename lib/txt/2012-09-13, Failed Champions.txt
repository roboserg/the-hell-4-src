ZERO HP GOD-MODE 
1. First make sure you are in possession of an item that gives you + hit points. 
2. Equip this item. 

2. Now cast blood star or bone spirit till your life gets to exact the value that your + hit points item is giving you. 
3. Cast mana shield. 
4. Take your + hit point item off. 

Your hit points should now reach 0. The character then is invincible to all forms of attack. A character with a hit point value of 0 will be killed by any sort of Healing. Be careful, before you go through a Portal or stairway, put your + hit points item back on, or you will see yourself dead in the next level or town.
